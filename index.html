How does BaliTripMate work?
+
BaliTripMate is an AI-powered bot on Telegram. After purchasing, you'll receive a license key via email. Open Telegram, find our Telegram bot, enter your key, and start chatting — just like texting a local friend!
Which languages does it support?
+
BaliTripMate supports 13+ languages including English, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Dutch, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Russian, Malay, Arabic, and Indonesian. You can also type in any other language!
Can I use it while already in Bali?
+
Absolutely! BaliTripMate is perfect for both pre-trip planning and real-time use while you're in Bali or Lombok. Ask for the nearest hidden gem, best restaurant nearby, or real-time tips anytime.
What if my license expires?
+
You'll receive a reminder before your plan expires. Simply purchase a new plan on Gumroad and get a fresh license key to continue using BaliTripMate.
Does it cover Lombok too?
+
Yes! BaliTripMate covers both Bali AND Lombok fully — from the Gili Islands to Mount Rinjani, hidden beaches, local restaurants, and everything in between.
Is there a refund policy?
+
We offer a 3-day refund if the bot has technical issues and is not working as described. Contact us via email and we'll resolve it promptly. Note: refunds are for technical issues only, not change of mind.
